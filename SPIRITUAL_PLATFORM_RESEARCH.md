### 15.6 Center/Ashram Locator Best Practices

Based on analysis of RSSB, Brahma Kumaris, Art of Living, Sadhguru, and Patanjali:
- Map-based interface with geolocation
- Filter by: city, state, district, pin code
- One-click driving directions (Google Maps/Apple Maps integration)
- Center details: address, phone, contact person, timings
- Event schedule per center
- "Nearest center" auto-detection via GPS
- Center-specific event calendar
- Photo gallery per center

### 15.7 Event Management Best Practices

- Calendar view with event types color-coded
- Registration directly in app (Art of Living model)
- Reminder notifications (1 day, 1 hour before)
- Live stream integration for virtual attendance
- Post-event content (recordings, photos)
- Recurring event support (weekly satsang)
- Capacity management and waitlists
- QR code check-in at physical events

### 15.8 Notification Strategy Best Practices

Based on highest-retention apps:
1. **Morning:** Daily wisdom quote/verse (consistent time)
2. **Practice Reminder:** User-selected time for meditation
3. **Streak Warning:** "Don't break your X-day streak!"
4. **Event Reminders:** 1 day and 1 hour before
5. **New Content:** Weekly content digest, not per-item
6. **Community:** When mentor/friend interacts
7. **Festivals:** Spiritual calendar notifications (Ekadashi, etc.)
8. **Opt-in always:** Follow Plum Village's consent-based approach
9. **Smart throttling:** Never more than 2-3 per day
10. **Personalization:** Based on user's practice patterns (Art of Living model)
